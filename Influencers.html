Influencers Guide: How It Works in Steps

At shoutouting.com, we connect micro-influencers with advertisers, providing you with a platform to share compelling messages across various social media platforms. Here's a step-by-step guide on how it works:

1
 Register and Fill Up the Form

Start your journey by registering on our user-friendly platform. Provide essential details and fill out a form to help us understand your interests and preferences.

2
Utilize Multiple Social Media Platforms

If you are active on more than one social media platform (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, Twitter), feel free to connect them all to your Shououting.com account. Maximize your reach and opportunities!

3
Sign In and Check for Offers

Once registered, sign in to your Shououting.com account. Keep an eye on your dashboard regularly to see if any exciting offers from advertisers are waiting for you.

4
Receive Detailed Instructionsn

Congratulations, an offer has arrived! Click on the offer to receive detailed instructions from the advertiser about what to share on your social media accounts. Pay close attention to the content, hashtags, and any specific guidelines provided.

5
Share on Your Social Media Accounts

Follow the instructions provided by the advertiser and share the designated content on your connected social media platforms. Your authentic voice and unique style make the message even more impactful.

6
Explore Publication and Await Rewards

After sharing the content, our team will review your publication to ensure it meets the requirements. If everything is in order, you can expect your well-deserved reward for the successful publication.
